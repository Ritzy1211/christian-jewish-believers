WHO WE ARE

We are Gentile Christians (All who have been called by the Lord our God – Acts 2:39.) We believe in identifying with our Jewish Root where Christianity emanates from through Jesus Christ (Yeshua Harmashia) a Jew. We believe, we are descendants (Legitimate Children of Abraham) through adoption of Jesus (Yeshua) to be part of Abraham’s family (The Jewish family) Galatians 3:7, 29 etc. We believe both Gentile Christians and Jews are identified family of God that worships “One God”. The Adonai, The Elohim (Deut. 6:4; Gen. 1:1; Num. 23:19; Psalm: 91:1). We are of Jewish based Christian faith through Jesus Christ (Yeshua Harmashia) We believe in the continuity of the Jewish root of the Christian faith through Jesus Christ (Yeshua Harmashia). We are not a church, but interdenominational Fellowship of Christian believers at all levels (General Overseers, Bishops, Apostles, Prophets etc.) who believes in their Jewish root where Christianity emanates from through Jesus.

OUR MISSION

To put on identical uniforms with Jesus/Yeshua’s brethren, (Jewish People) as one unified family of God that worship the same one God. The Elohim, The Adonai. To serve as a bridge building between the Jews and Gentile Christians as one unified family of God. (Ephesians 2:11-15; 3:5-6 etc.) To Organize Evangelical Revival meetings, Crusades, Seminars, Symposiums, reaching out to Schools, Para-Military outfits etc. To be involved in supporting projects in Israel.

OUR OBJECTIVE

Our objective is to educate the Gentile Christians all over the world to know their primary responsibilities to the brethren of Jesus (the Jewish people) in accordance to God’s commandments, based on Biblical injunctions.

CJB STATEMENT OF FAITH

We believe God the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit is ONE GOD. We believe in the Bible as the whole counsel of God to the church and to the world at large. We believe in the unity of the body of Christ and the total support for the Nation of Israel and her values.
