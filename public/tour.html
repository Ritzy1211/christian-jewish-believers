Tour to the Holy Land

Walk where the scriptures came alive. Join our guided pilgrimages.



Pilgrimage Tour to the Holy Land of Israel is highly Revealing, Educative, Inspiring and Bible based. Join the President – Bishop Dr. Golden Kerewi to Israel for a Spiritual Retreat and for Educational purpose. This will accord you the privilege to know and understand the significance of visit to the Holy Land and how it connects to Salvation, Eternal life, Rapture and the 2nd coming of the Lord Jesus Christ. If you are eternity minded and hoping for eternal life to reign eternally with God (Matt 19:16), follow the President to Israel for practical teachings on Eschatology from the Bible. Be sure you will return from the tour to your country blessed in all Spiritual Sphere.
