Messianic School of Eschatology & Jewish Root

A place of Godly Knowledge.

What You’ll Learn

Our curriculum explores biblical prophecy, the unity of Israel and the Church, and end-time signs from a scriptural perspective.

Preparing students (Believing Christians) to understand the past, present &future signs(Events) of the end times to help them know the times we are in and to build them to prepare themselves for RAPTURE- in the coming of Jesus the Yeshua in the air (Thess 4:16,17, etc.) to take his perfect ( spiritual matured) Brides/saints to be with him in Heaven. The event will be fulfilled with the church made up of Jews and Gentiles Christians (Ephesians 2:1-15; 3:5-6 etc.) Also introducing the students (believing Christians) to the Jewish Background of the New Testaments in reference to the nation of Israel.

JEWISH ROOT: (6 months)



This course will; 1. Will look at the importance of Jewish identity and examine the New Testament in light of the Old Testament background. ​ 2. Will look into the Jewish world of Jesus and His followers and the subsequent development of Christianity. ​ 3. Will introduce the student to the Jewish background of the New Testament in reference to the Nation of Israel, 4. Will examine what the New Testament says about Gods promises to the Jewish people & the Land of Israel. ​ 5. Will examine antisemitism toward the Jews & Christianity ​ 6. Will examine conflict resolution between Judaism Jews & Christianity in light of Christ commandments to the church as requisite for eternity with God. ​

The aim of this course:

1. Is to enable the student understand Jesus life in its Jewish context and to correct some of the misunderstandings about Him and His world in order to have an accurate basis for our theology & understand of history. 2. To help the student to know his Jewish root as descendant of Abraham the progenitor Of the Jewish race by adoption through Jesus Christ to be one unified Jewish family of God to live together with the goal to love each other in peace & unity to reduce antisemitism to a minimum level towards each other.
