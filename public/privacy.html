Privacy Policy

Your trust matters. Here’s how we handle your data.

1. Introduction

Christian Jewish Believers (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information you provide directly such as name, email address, business details, and messages via forms.

3. How We Use Information

To respond to inquiries, process registrations and donations, send updates, and improve our services.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your information. We may share it with trusted partners to fulfill services (e.g., email sending providers).

5. Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure.

6. Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

7. Contact

For questions about this policy, email us at info@cjb.org.
